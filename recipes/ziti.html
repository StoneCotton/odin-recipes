Baked Ziti

Description

This is a delicious dinner that is easy to make and has a lot of vegetables.

This baked ziti is always a hit! The croutons on top give it that crunchy dimension that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of ziti
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of croutons

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook ziti according to package directions.
  3. Mix marinara sauce, ricotta cheese, and mozzarella cheese in a large bowl.
  4. Add ziti to the bowl and mix until pasta is coated.
  5. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish and top with croutons.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes.
